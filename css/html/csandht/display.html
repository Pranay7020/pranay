Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the greatest footballers of all time.

He was born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal.

From a young age, he showed great talent in football.

He began his professional career at Sporting CP.

Soon, he was signed by Manchester United in 2003.

At United, he developed into a world-class player.

He won three Premier League titles and a Champions League with the club.

In 2009, he moved to Real Madrid for a record fee.

At Madrid, he broke many records and won four more Champions League titles.

He scored over 450 goals for the club.

Ronaldo later played for Juventus and returned to Manchester United.

He is known for his speed, skill, and powerful shots.
